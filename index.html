About Us

Mail Express is your complete mail center. We proudly serve individuals and businesses with a variety of convenient services including printing, shipping, and secure document handling.

Our friendly team is always here to help you get your tasks done faster and smarter.

Our Services

Shipping & Mailing

Fast, reliable shipping with all major carriers – USPS, UPS, FedEx. Whether it’s across town or across the globe, we’ll help you choose the best option for your timeline and budget.

Packaging

From bubble wrap to custom boxes, we professionally pack your items with care. Fragile (artwork, frames, glassware, ceramics) or heavy duty (camp trunks, luggage)—we’ve got you covered. Over 50 box sizes available, plus custom sizing on request.

Printing

Black & white or color printing for documents, flyers, resumes, and more. Expect high-quality results and fast turnaround every time.

Lamination

Protect important documents and ID cards with durable lamination. Available in a variety of sizes.

Book Binding

Give your presentations, reports, or projects a professional touch with clean, sturdy binding options.

Notary Services

Licensed notary available on-site daily for quick notarizations. Virtual notarizations now available as well.

Mailbox Rental

Secure a private mailbox with a real street address—ideal for businesses and frequent travelers. Receive packages from all carriers with 24/7 access.

Passport Photos

Professional, government-compliant passport and ID photos printed on the spot—no appointment needed.

Secure Shredding

Safeguard your privacy with secure document shredding, in partnership with ShredAmerica. NAID AAA Certified and HIPAA Compliant.

Greeting Cards & Gift Wrap

Last-minute gift? We’ve got you covered with curated greeting cards and elegant gift wrapping options.

Key Duplication

Fast and accurate key copies while you wait—for home, office, or spare sets.
